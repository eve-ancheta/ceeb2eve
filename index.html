Believe in Eve

I was a freshman in homeschool–high school, someone who'd never talked to another teen, someone who knew nothing about the world and so much less about themself. Since that time, I've taken my first graded class, joined my first teenage friend group, led data science, advocacy and homeschool organizations and got into Stanford, and now, that's changed.
